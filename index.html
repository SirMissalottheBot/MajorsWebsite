My Dog Major

Who is Major?

Major is seven year old border collie, from working stock. Major's sire was a sheepdog trial champion. He has short hair and loves chasing the flirt pole or playing fetch in the local field.

Major likes dogs more than people and gets excited whenever he meets new four‑legged friends on his walks. After a long run, he enjoys kicking back and relaxing in the local pub, where he happily curls up under the table. He’s a loyal, fun dog who brings a lot of joy to everyone who meets him.

Major's top 3 favorite foods

  1. Gravy bones
  2. DentiSticks
  3. Roast Chicken
